## XCEED Event Creation

Redesigned the first-event publishing flow to remove premature commitment and improve organizer activation. The core issue was simple: users had to commit before they had enough confidence, especially through mandatory venue setup, irreversible details, and blocking steps.

## What I changed

I restructured the flow around progressive disclosure, reduced unnecessary onboarding friction, kept event editing available throughout, and simplified admission setup. The goal was to help organizers get an event live first, then refine it later.

## Product thinking

The hypothesis was that focusing early steps on only what is essential to go live would improve activation without hurting listing quality.

## Expected outcome

Faster time to first published event, better venue-to-publish conversion, and fewer stalled setups.
