# Designing Safe, Low-Distraction Vehicle Dashboard

<Org org="mb" />

Modern car interfaces are still early in their digital evolution. Some manufacturers try to replicate smartphone UX. Others experiment with their own interaction models.

After owning a Mercedes for more than five years, I noted many issues in the daily experience of the dashboard. That observation led me to explore what a better in-car interface could look like.

In this case study, I analyze modern premium automotive dashboards and design an improved UX for Mercedes. The goal is simple: improve safety, reduce task completion time, and create a visual system worthy of the brand.
